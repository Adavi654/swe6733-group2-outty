## Product Vision Statement

### Far Vision

Outty will become the leading adventure-focused social platform which will allow outdoor lovers
to make important connections based on shared interests, compatible skill levels, and passion
for exploration. Unlike traditional dating applications, Outty will focus on building genuine
relationships through outdoor experiences and adventures.

### Near Vision

Develop a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) that allows users to:
- Register and log in to the platform
- Create and manage adventure profiles
- Select adventure interests and skill levels
- Discover nearby users with similar interests
- Swipe right or left on possible adventure partners to show interest or disinterest
- Generate matches when interest is mutual
- Exchange messages with matched users
